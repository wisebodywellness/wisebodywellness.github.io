Annie Ellis-West, M.Ed.

Holistic Nutrition Specialist

Intuitive Eating Coach

Registered Yoga Teacher

Born in Aspen, Colorado on a cold winter’s morning and raised in Vermont by a single mother; Annie is a foodie, devoted yogi and lover of the natural world. She was raised in the natural food co-op in the 1970’s and ’80’s, where she was surrounded by people who had vegetable gardens, drank raw milk and made bread from scratch. Nutrition “talk” was in the air long before it was trendy or mainstream. Label reading was an early skill and knowledge of undesirable food additives and practices was always part of the conversation. You might say that wise eating is in Annie’s cells from an early age. As an adult Annie became keenly aware of her body’s reactions to foods when she fell in love with yoga and wanted her body always functioning optimally thus allowing her to practice yoga with the most strength and energy possible.

A lifetime of nutrition knowledge combined with the yogini’s experience of deep listening and honoring the body’s messages provides Annie with a robust skill set for her coaching. Our history with food and current eating habits are often intricately woven together. I coach you through discovering some of the root causes of current eating habits and help you bring your life into balance. The techniques I teach are simple, empowering and easy to use, they can be used to re-balance your life no matter where you are in your day or in the world.
